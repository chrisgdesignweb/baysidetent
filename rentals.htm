Misc. Rentals

At Bayside Tent, we carry a wide range of miscellaneous equipment that we continue to add to each year. We have many styles of tables and chairs for all of your event needs. We have a new line of dance floors called New England Plank- a very popular and great looking style floor.

We have a wide variety of china, glassware, silverware and linens to choose from. We also have other equipment available upon request. Please call or fill out an estimate form for availability and pricing. If we do not carry a particular item to suit your needs, we’ll make every effort to locate it for you.

We carry the following:

  • Tables
  • Chairs
  • Dance Floors
  • Staging
  • Lighting
  • Linens
  • China
  • Glassware
  • Flatware
  • Heaters
