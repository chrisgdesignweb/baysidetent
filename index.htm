Welcome to Cape Cod’s Premier Tent Company. Let us help make your wedding, party or event magical and memorable.



At Bayside Tent, our first priority is always the customer. We pride ourselves on maintaining superior customer service. We understand that your wedding, party or special event requires attention to detail and needs to be perfect. Our primary goal is perfection for your special event.


Our superior customer service and dedication of our workers has enabled us to gain the confidence of our customers especially but also the top resorts, caterers and event planners on Cape Cod, the South Shore and Southeastern, MA. We take the work out of your event! We’re experienced at creating, managing and organizing events of all types and sizes.
