About Bayside Tent

At Bayside Tent, we take pride in making sure that your event is successful. From beginning to end, we’ll work with you to make sure everything is perfect. Bayside Tent continues to add new items regularly to its inventory to help better service our customer’s party needs.

Bayside Tent has a new owner, Ryan Gillis. Before joining Bayside Tent, Gillis owned a successful Public Relations/Event Management firm. He has created and managed many special events for corporations, celebrities, athletes and media. He’s been involved in the tent and equipment rental business for twenty years as well. Gillis started as a tent installer during his college days.
“It’s crazy how life works. I worked as a tent installer in my teens and twenties, founded and helped run a very successful PR/Event Management Company and now I’m back swinging a sledge hammer. I love it. My business experience really comes in handy here though- especially the event management side.”

Gillis lives with his wife Leah and daughter Rylie in Dennisport, MA.
