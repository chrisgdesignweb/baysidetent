Tents

Celebrate in the fresh air while we take care of the weather

Bayside Tent and Table continues to add new inventory for the 2016 spring and summer season ensuring that the tent you choose will be of the highest quality. Our Eureka Genesis rope tents are enormously popular for wedding functions of all sizes.

We also carry a full line of frame tents. Sturdy and dependable, the frame tents can be assembled in almost any area. Frame tents are often recommended when space limitations are a factor (decks, patios, etc.) but they are expandable and can accommodate groups of many sizes.

We offer tents with style, performance and durability -- made with quality craftsmanship. These tents offer proven performance in almost all weather and are certified to meet building, structural and fire safety codes. Our tent sizes range from 10x10 to 60x150. We have you covered!

Sidewall for our tents are always available as well. We carry clear, solid and cathedral (window) sidewall if needed.
